Peach Chat Terms and Community Guidelines

Effective date: 27 June 2026

By using Peach Chat, you agree to follow these Terms and Community Guidelines. If you do not agree, do not use the app.

Age requirement

Peach Chat is intended only for users aged 18 and above. By using the app, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old.

User accounts

Users are responsible for keeping their login details secure. Do not impersonate another person, create accounts for abuse, or use the app for scams, spam, or harassment.

Prohibited content and behavior

  • Harassment, threats, hate, bullying, or abusive messages
  • Sexual content involving minors, child sexual abuse material, grooming, or exploitation
  • Illegal content or instructions for illegal activity
  • Graphic violence, self-harm encouragement, or dangerous content
  • Spam, scams, fraud, phishing, or misleading activity
  • Private information of another person without permission
  • Unwanted sexual messages, coercion, exploitation, or blackmail
  • Content that violates another person’s rights

User-generated content

Messages, photos, profile details, and reactions shared by users are user-generated content. Users are responsible for the content they share.

Reporting and blocking

Users can report or block other users inside the app. Reports may be reviewed and action may be taken where these guidelines are violated.

Moderation

Peach Chat may remove content, restrict accounts, suspend accounts, or delete accounts that violate these Terms or Community Guidelines.

Account deletion

Users may request deletion inside the app from Profile > Delete Peach Chat Account & Data or through the public account deletion page.
