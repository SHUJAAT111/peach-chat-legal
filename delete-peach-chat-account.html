Delete Peach Chat Account

Use this page to request deletion of your Peach Chat account and associated data.

How to request deletion

Send an email to Peach Chat support with your Peach Chat Login ID.

Email deletion request

Information to include

  • Peach Chat Login ID
  • Approximate account creation date, if known
  • Reason for deletion, optional

What will be deleted or anonymized

  • Peach Chat account/profile information
  • Profile image where applicable
  • Notification tokens
  • Local app data after in-app deletion
  • Temporary chat images associated with the account where technically possible

Some limited records may be retained only when necessary for safety, abuse prevention, legal compliance, or dispute resolution.

In-app deletion

You can also delete from inside Peach Chat: Profile > Delete Peach Chat Account & Data.
