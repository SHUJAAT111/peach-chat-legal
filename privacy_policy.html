Peach Chat Privacy Policy

Effective date: 27 June 2026

Peach Chat is a social chat application for adults. It allows users to create a profile, find other users, exchange messages, share images, and receive notifications.

Information we collect

  • Login ID and password authentication information
  • Profile information such as nickname, age, gender, country, optional bio, optional height, optional body type, optional favorite color, and profile picture or avatar
  • Chat messages, reactions, read status, typing status, and chat images
  • Blocked user records and user reports
  • Firebase user ID, notification token, online status, last seen time, and app timestamps
  • Technical information needed to operate, secure, and maintain the app

Peach Chat does not require phone number or email registration from users.

How we use information

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To show profiles and search results
  • To deliver real-time messages, pictures, typing indicators, read status, and reactions
  • To send push notifications when enabled
  • To provide report and block tools
  • To prevent abuse, spam, scams, harassment, underage use, and policy violations
  • To maintain app security, reliability, and functionality

User-generated content

Messages, profile details, reactions, and photos shared by users are user-generated content. Users must not share illegal, abusive, threatening, hateful, sexually exploitative, spam, scam, or unsafe content. Content involving minors, child sexual abuse material, grooming, exploitation, or sexual solicitation of minors is strictly prohibited.

Chat images and one-time images

One-time images are designed to be hidden after viewing. Shared chat images may be automatically removed after a limited time. Technical delays may occur, but the app is designed to reduce long-term storage of temporary shared images.

Data storage and service providers

Peach Chat uses Firebase services for authentication, database, storage, and push notifications. Firebase/Google cloud services may process data as required to operate the app. We do not sell user data.

Reporting and blocking

Users can report or block other users inside Peach Chat. Reports may be reviewed for safety enforcement. Action may include warnings, restrictions, content removal, or account removal.

Account deletion

Users can request deletion inside the app from Profile > Delete Peach Chat Account & Data. Users may also request deletion through the public account deletion page. When an account is deleted, Peach Chat removes or anonymizes the account/profile information, clears notification tokens, removes local app data, and requests deletion of account data where technically possible. Some limited records may be retained only where necessary for security, abuse prevention, legal compliance, or dispute resolution.

Account deletion page: publish the included delete-peach-chat-account.html page and place its public URL in Google Play Console.

Children

Peach Chat is intended only for users aged 18 and above. The app is not directed to children.
